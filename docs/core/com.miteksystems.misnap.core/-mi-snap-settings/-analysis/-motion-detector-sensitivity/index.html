MotionDetectorSensitivity

enum MotionDetectorSensitivity : Enum<MiSnapSettings.Analysis.MotionDetectorSensitivity>

Describes the different levels of sensitivity for the device motion detector.

  • NONE - No motion detection is performed.

  • LOW - The motion detector is less sensitive to movement allowing for more movement but being less effective on preventing blurry frames.

  • HIGH - The motion detector is more sensitive to movement allowing for less movement but being more effective on preventing blurry frames.
